About Us

Vision

Established in January of 2004, Detroit Barbell is operated by Clay Brandenburg and JJ Thomas. The gym was opened with the intent of bringing together the area's strongest lifters. The gym provides a supportive and educational environment for any serious athlete.

Methodology

With over 20 years of combined experience and knowledge, Detroit Barbell has the ability to take your training to the next level. Detroit Barbell's approach to training is based on the Westside Barbell Conjugate Training methodology. These methods are used by Detroit Barbell members and have proven success.
