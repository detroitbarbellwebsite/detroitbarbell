Our Mission

Welcome to Detroit Barbell Strength Training Facility, or Detroit Barbell for short. Detroit Barbell is located just south of Detroit, Michigan.

The gym’s primary function is powerlifting, but accommodates the needs of any serious athlete. Detroit Barbell has all levels of lifters, from amateur to elite level pro powerlifters.

Relentless Detroit

Relentless Detroit is a U.P.A sanctioned powerlifting meet that consist of over 100 men and women competing in the bench, deadlift and squat for a total in their respective weight classes and Divisions.
